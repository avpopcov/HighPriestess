Workflow

We align on your goals via a quick questionnaire, email, or a Zoom call.

Whether you already have a strict brief and script, or need me to develop the creative concept and script from scratch – I've got you covered.

To lock in the project, we sign a contract and outline the exact deliverables.

A 50% upfront deposit is required to begin, and the remaining 50% is paid upon final approval of the content.

Products are shipped, and production officially kicks off!

I handle the complete creation cycle: scripting, styling, professional lighting, recording with pro-gear, and high-conversion post-production editing.

  • Turnaround: First drafts are delivered within 10 business days after receiving the products.
  • Revisions: Included edits will be reviewed and adjusted to perfection.
  • Delivery: Final high-quality files are sent via Google Drive or WeTransfer.
