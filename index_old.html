About Me

  • 1. Vision & Briefing

    We align on your goals via a quick questionnaire, email, or a Zoom call. Whether you already have a strict brief and script, or need me to develop the creative concept and script from scratch—I’ve got you covered.

  • 2. Agreement & Deposit

    To lock in the project, we sign a contract, and a 50% upfront deposit is required. The remaining 50% is paid upon final approval of the content.

  • Production & Delivery

    Turnaround: First drafts are delivered within 10 business days after receiving the products.
    Revisions: Included edits will be reviewed and adjusted to perfection.
    Delivery: Final high-quality files are sent via Google Drive or WeTransfer.
